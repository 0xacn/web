Overview

Never forget anything again. The ideal way to store information into your long-term memory is to space out recall attempts just right.

You can publish your cards and decks to be used by anyone. You can rate and review public cards and decks to give authors feedback on what they can improve.

An advanced card editor works with LaTeX, Markdown, code highlighting, and audio. Includes a simple preview mechanism.
